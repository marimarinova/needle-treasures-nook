About DMC and Anchor Thread Conversion

DMC and Anchor are the two most popular brands of 6-strand cotton embroidery floss used in cross stitch and embroidery. While both brands offer high-quality threads with excellent colorfastness, their numbering systems are completely different.

Important notes about thread conversion:

  • Colors are approximate matches - no two brands produce identical colors
  • Always complete a project using threads from the same manufacturer to ensure color consistency
  • Some DMC colors have no exact Anchor equivalent and vice versa
  • When multiple Anchor codes are listed, they represent the closest matches

Why convert between brands?

  • Your local store only carries one brand
  • A color is out of stock in your preferred brand
  • You have leftover floss from another brand you want to use
  • Pattern calls for a brand you don't typically use

This conversion chart is based on data from Yarn Tree, 123Stitch.com, and verified by the cross stitch community. For the most accurate results, always compare actual thread colors before starting a project.
