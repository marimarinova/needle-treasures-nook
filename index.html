A Needle, A Thread, A Story

Over thirty years pulling thread through fabric. What started as a quiet craft by the window became something more — a small studio where patterns are born from morning walks, pressed flowers, and the kind of beauty you find when you're not looking too hard.

Every design is stitched by hand first, on linen I dye myself with tea, marigolds, and whatever the garden offers. Modern primitive, they call it. I just call it home.

— Marieta, Needle Treasures Nook
