A Needle, A Thread, A Story

Thirty years. That's how long I've been pulling thread through fabric. Long enough to know that cross-stitch isn't really about the finished piece ‚Äî it's about the doing. The stillness. The way an hour disappears when your hands are busy and your mind is free.

I'm Marieta, and I create patterns for people like me: the ones who'd rather stitch by a window than scroll through a screen. The ones with a stack of unread books that somehow keeps growing. The ones who notice things ‚Äî the way lichen maps itself across stone, or how dried flowers hold their shape long after their color fades.

My work is primitive in spirit. Simple shapes. Earthy palettes. Designs that look like they could have been stitched a hundred years ago, on linen stained with tea and time. Nothing precious. Nothing perfect. Just honest work for quiet hands.

I stitch on hand-dyed fabrics ‚Äî tea, coffee, herbs from the garden ‚Äî but my patterns work beautifully on whatever you love. The PDF downloads instantly. The rest is up to you and a long afternoon.

Take your time,
Marieta
